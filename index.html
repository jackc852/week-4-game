You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four jewels below. Each of them has been assigned a different value. Clicking on them will reveal their value.

Each click of a jewel will add to your total value. Win by matching your total value to the random number. If your total exceeds the random number, you lose.

Each win or loss will restart the game, and each value will reset every time the game restarts.

Good luck!
